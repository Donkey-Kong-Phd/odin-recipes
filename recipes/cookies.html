Cookies

Description

Indulge in the delightful world of Snickerdoodles - a classic, melt-in-your-mouth treat that's sure to delight your taste buds. These soft and chewy cookies are a perfect blend of sweetness and spice, generously rolled in a cinnamon-sugar coating that adds a magical touch to every bite. With a slightly tangy flavor and a buttery aroma, Snickerdoodles offer a comforting and nostalgic experience that instantly transports you to the warmth of grandma's kitchen.

Cookies are great to accompany any kind of meal. You can eat them by themselves, by yourself, or with other people. These cookies are really addictive, so be careful! My grandma used to bake something like them.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Steps

  • Step 1

    Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease.

  • Step 2

    Make cookies: Beat together white sugar, butter, and eggs in a large bowl using an electric mixer until smooth and creamy. Combine flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt in a separate bowl; stir into creamed butter mixture until dough holds together.

  • Step 3

    Mix together white sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl until well combined.

  • Step 4

    Form dough into balls (about 2 teaspoons in each ball), then roll in cinnamon-sugar coating. Place dough balls about 2 inches apart onto the prepared baking sheet.

  • Step 5

    Bake in the preheated oven on the center rack for 7 minutes. Allow cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
